	Name
	Sex: male/female
	Age
	# Legs: 2/4
	Color
	Method: says (some noise)

	Spots: yes/no
	Dairy/Slaughter
	Method: is ready for (milking/slaughter)

	Riding/Show
	Method: is currently in the (field/stable/etc)

Pig:
	Bacon/Ham/Sausage
	Method: is (eating/sleeping/mudding)

Chicken:
	Number of eggs for the day
	Method: is currently in the (coop/feed area/etc)
